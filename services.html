Our Core Services

Movement Therapy, Handwriting Support, Parent Therapy & More

What We Offer

Movement Therapy

RIPED Movement Training & exercises to help children overcome dyspraxia, improve coordination, and build confidence.

Handwriting Support

Specialized drills & fine motor skill practice for children with dyslexia, motor planning issues, or writing anxiety.

Parent Therapy

Stress management, EFT, and nurturing massage techniques to help parents remain calm & supportive.

Rocking Therapy

A gentle, rhythmic approach to soothe birth trauma, reduce fear responses, and promote relaxation in children.

Our integrated approach ensures that every child and parent receives a plan tailored to their unique challenges. Ready to learn more? Contact us to discuss a personalized plan for your child.
