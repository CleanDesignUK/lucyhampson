About Lucy Hampson

Movement Therapy, Handwriting Support & More

Who We Are

Lucy Hampson is dedicated to helping children with dyspraxia, dyslexia, autism, and related conditions. By integrating movement therapy, handwriting support, and parent-focused care, we address both the physical and emotional aspects of development.

Our holistic approach embraces each child’s strengths while providing targeted interventions for areas of need. We empower parents to continue therapy at home, ensuring that progress made in sessions translates into daily life.

Our Mission

We believe in empowering children to reach their full potential by offering comprehensive programs addressing motor skills, handwriting proficiency, and emotional well-being. Through collaboration with families, we foster growth, resilience, and confidence in every child we serve.
